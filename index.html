Splootie, Gift & The Letter

The door had burst open, and to his surprise, Hagrid, Santa and Hegwig were in his living room parading a sealed letter for Priya. Hagrid told Splootie that he was the only one who could get this important letter to Priya (which was great, as Splootie wanted to go to see Priya, so it was a win-win). Santa was just being lazy this Christmas and wanted give Priya her present via Splootie. So Splootie quickly got up and got his stuff packed, carrying the essential items, such as my homeless stick, the letter and bag, and set off to see Priya for Christmas!
